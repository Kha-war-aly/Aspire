Keep Your

Unlocking the Potential of Tomorrow’s Leaders.

At A2A Foundation, we are committed to empowering and supporting the dreams of student athletes who display exceptional dedication and determination in both academics and athletics.

Our Student Athlete Sponsorship Program is designed to provide deserving individuals with the necessary support, resources, and opportunities to thrive on and off the sports field. With access to tutoring services, school supplies, andsports equipment, our program provides a pathway for success and helps young individuals overcome adversities and discover their full potential.

Team Sponsorship Program:

Our Team Sponsorship Program directly contributes toyour team’s success by providing financial assistance for equipment, travel expenses, and other essential needs.

How to Apply:

All interested organizations must submit an application in order to be considered for team sponsorship.

All Organizations must meet the following criteria:
Your organization directly serves youth between the ages of 6-18 years old.

Your organization is located in Nevada.

Finally, please note the following:

  • It is recommended that you print a hard copy of your application for your own records.
  • Make sure you are using the most updated version of your web browser when completing the application.
  • If you have questions, issues, or concerns regarding this process, please contact us directly via email/phone call.
