OUR MISSION

About Us

Aspire 2 Ascend Foundation works to empower at-risk student athletes by providing free clinics and financial sponsorship to cover expenses associated with their sport.

We are committed to the development of student athletes, equally through the enhancement of life skills as well as the improvement of athletic and academic performance.

Sports and physical activity can make a huge difference in a child’s life. Unfortunately, not every family can afford the gear, leagues, and travel costs associated with youth sports.

That’s where Aspire 2 Ascend comes in - we’re dedicated to making sure ALL kids have the opportunity to play and stay active!

Sports and physical activity can make a huge difference in a child’s life.

Who is A2a For?

We work with organizations in high-need communities, serving kids ages 6-18.

Playing sports not only helps kids stay fit and healthy, but it also promotes teamwork and builds self-confidence. So let’s give every child the chance to get off the sidelines and onto the field. Let’s level the playing field with Aspire 2 Ascend

The founders

Our story...

We know firsthand how amazing sports can be for kids, especially when growing up in a single-parent household. We were lucky enough to have sponsors that supported our love for sports and helped us become successful in life.

Now, as parents, we want to give back and support our own son and other kids who might not have had that same opportunity.

Let’s work together and make sure every child has the chance to experience the power of sports!
