Anthony Gigliotti

PRESIDENT

Meet Anthony Gigliotti, the passionate and dedicated president of Aspire 2 Ascend Foundation.

With a deep love for sports and education and a strong belief in its transformative power, Anthony founded the organization with the mission to provide at-risk and under resourced student athletes with opportunities they deserve. Raised in Prince George’s County Maryland,Anthony spent his childhood participating in sports.

Growing up in a single parent household, he experienced first-hand, the important role financial sponsorship programs played in easing the financial burden for families with limited resources.

Through free athletic camps, community health fairs, equipment grants, and sports scholarships, Anthony and his team are tirelessly working to break down financial barriers for student athletes and instill discipline, teamwork, and leadership, empowering them to overcome challenges both on and off the sports field.

Sarah Gigliotti

DIRECTOR & TREASURER

Introducing Sarah Gigliotti, the Director & Treasurer of Aspire 2 Ascend Foundation.

With a captivating presence and a heart for social impact, Sarah has been instrumental in shaping the organization’s vision and driving its success.

Her unwavering commitment to children’s well being have inspired community partners and donors to join the cause. Through her strategic financial management, Sarah ensures that every dollar is optimally allocated to fund programs for at risk and disadvantaged student athletes.

As the face of the foundation, Sarah’s enthusiasm and genuine compassion resonate deeply with the community, making her a true champion for their brighter future.
